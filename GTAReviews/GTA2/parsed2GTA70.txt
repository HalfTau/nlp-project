Grand Theft Auto 2 took Grand Theft Auto classic concepts and made it better. Cars now have even more and better weapons. You werent satisfied with just running over es, hoes, and pedestrians in the prequel? You now can electrocute them with your electro gun. The radio is chock full of satire and hilarity. There are even more varied ways to earn cash and respect in the game. To top it all off, the graphics for Grand Theft Auto 2 is a massive upgrade from its predecessor. Everything is much more detailed which in turn makes the explosions and gores that much more satisfying. If youre going to play a classic Grand Theft Auto game, might as well start at number 2 and skip the first one entirely.
