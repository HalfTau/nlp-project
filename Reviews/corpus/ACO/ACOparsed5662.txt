Assassins Creed Breath of the Wild is a really good game with lots of freedom. There are multiple melee weapons, shields, and bows! There are also tools and you can hunt for materials. Also, there are many dungeons to explore! You have different mounts with a huge world to explore, spanning many miles. There are sidequests and a main quests, and you can take down most of the bosses in pretty much any order you choose!
