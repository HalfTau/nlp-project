This is an amazingly deep game that you need to buy. The combat may feel clunky at first, but that is all part of getting a feel for each weapon classs abiltiies and benefits. The environments and NPCs are so well developed, there is something interesting at almost every turn, and the world feels incredibly alive. The main story is engaging and progression is natural, not a grind. Do what you want and you will progress well.GET THIS GAME!
