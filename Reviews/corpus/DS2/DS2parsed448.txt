This masterpiece right here has to be one of the best games Ive ever played, Without a Doubt. It has an Amazing Storyline, Brilliant Gameplay and Excellent Dialogue. And this is coming from a guy who isnt a huge fan of RPGs. This game will bring hours of Excitment and will leave you hanging on the edge of your seat. Huge game from mine and many other peoples childhoods so if youre lucky like me, This game will bring a great amount of pure nostalgia.10/10
