The final chapter of Ezio Auditore, and what a truly amazing journey he has had. This installment, although called Revelations, opens up knew doors in the magnificant story of the assassins and the templers. Completely meddling with your already muddled mind. With all the favorourite assassin abilities, such as air assassinations, free running, throwing knifes and the hidden blade Revelations continues with Brotherhood ability to call on other assassins when you are in need and adds the abiity to use ziplines with your new Hook blade. The game does get a little bit repetative towards the end, but if you love the series you will love this installment. 8.5/10 Ubisoft,  Bring on Creed 3. And start explaining everthing.
