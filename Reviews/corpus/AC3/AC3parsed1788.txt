Although there  are many mixed reviews about this game, overall it is still worth a shot to play through. If you loved the gameplay from Assassins Creed 2, you will find the same game mechanics in this game along with better additions. Anyone who has played the Assassins Creed games, will surely enjoy this one.No other Assassins Creed game tells a more compelling story. Although it is long and slow going, the expierience is worth it.
