Assassins Creed III is one of the most popular AC games released.The introduction of two new playable characters, locations, story and features makes it a large move forward from Assassins Creed Revelations.The new dual weapon combat system adds new fighting techniques and finishes without altering the much loved current combat system.AC III adds the new hunting and sailing features which gave way to Assassins Creed IV Black Flag.Added side quests and collectables make it the perfect type of game for people who enjoy working towards 100 completion in all aspects.The new story takes a break from middle millenia Europe and moves to 18th Century Colonial America which creates a whole new atmosphere and experience in all aspects.
