The PC version of Assassins Creed III has been injected with eyepopping enhancements that bring its compelling story to life. Its like personally participating in the American Revolution without the consequences of death. The ticket price of traveling back to the fascinating period of Americas battle for independence, and the fictional story that Ubisoft Montpellier has layered within it, is just a copy of Assassins Creed III and a highend PC. Just remember that youll have to leave your social life behind.
