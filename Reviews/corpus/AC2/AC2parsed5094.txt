Probably the best Assassins Creed game to date. It made huge leaps from its predecessor, in every area. The graphics are still very good, even though this game came out a few years ago and as usual, music and voice acting is superb. The story is probably the best of all the other games in the franchise, simply because Ezio actually has friends and family around other games depict him as more solemn person who works alone. Its very immersive, the crouds felt very real and the enviroments were lifelike. As always, combat is very fun again, it probably beats the other games the first was very hard while the rest are very easy.Buy this game now! Its very cheap on sale, and gives well over 20 hours of gameplay.
