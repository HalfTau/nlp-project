Often thought of as the highlight of the AC series, and for a good reason. This game builds on the promise of the original to bring you a fun modern platformer. If youre a fan of the original, or games like Arkham Asylum, you owe it to yourself to pick up AC II!The plot is nothing groundbreaking but its strong enough to draw you in. and the action the real reason to play this game is terrific. A solid gaming experiance overall. Just dont be thrown off by the title and expect a stealth game in line with the classic Thef titles.
