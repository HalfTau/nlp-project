The setting is really beautiful and if youre a history geek you will love all the cameos by famous dead Italians.  Ezio is prone to jumping in a direction perpendicular to your intended trajectory, and the gameplay is really repetitive, but its fun walking around 15th century Italy.  Also at one point a woman will challenge you to a horse race and if you win she will immediately get on top of you and have sex with you, right on the ground, in the middle of the day, in front of dozens of people.
