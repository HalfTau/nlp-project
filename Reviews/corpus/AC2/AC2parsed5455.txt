AC2 takes all the cool stuff from AC1 the historical scenary, stealth aspects, etc and further distills them into a digital drug thats hard to resist since its now tempered by a much more varied gameplay and a story that doesnt leave you with more questions than answers.  The current 30EUR pricetag makes this a mustbuy if you like stealth/action games.  Personally, I could do without the Tombraider moments during some sequences in the game where the awkward camera angles makes some jumps much harder than they should be, but they do offer a reprieve from all the disemboweling P
