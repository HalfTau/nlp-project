Assassins Creed 2 takes the best aspects of the original Asassins Creed and perfects them, creating a masterpiece.You now have real open world gameplay, the story is more humane and emotionally engaging, and you get to visit beautiful cities of Renaissance Italy.If you didnt like certain aspects of the first title, youll love this game and if youre not a fan of the AC series, now is the time to start.
