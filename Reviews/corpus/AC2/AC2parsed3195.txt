ACII is a lengthy game for sure, unexpectedly time consuming with its vast cityscapes and distracting amount of side content and toys to play with. The game does a good job at intertwining history with historical fiction, but the overall story is weak, as is the writing and the combat. However the base gameplay is enough fun to distract from these issues. Theres always an opportunity to take a break and mess with some guards, jump off huge buildings, chase burglars, kick people off rooftops, or laugh at a few glitches and bugs. I once got stuck to my horse and everybody watched...
