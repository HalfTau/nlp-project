Assassins Creed II is a better game than its forebear and is a beautiful and memorable experience on its own terms. The experience is layered, unique, and shows an incredible attention to detail. However, the controls are exceptionally annoying as they are depicted as pictures, and you are meant to work out what they are on the keyboard or mouse. Other than the controls issue, No matter what your preconceptions are about Assassins Creed II, the game is well worth your time and money.
