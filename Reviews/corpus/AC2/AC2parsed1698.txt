This game is the for such an old game the landscapes and settings are much more beautiful than even current gen games. Whilst the missions can get repetitive, its still fun to just unwind, explore, climb buildings and collect randomto make your mansion look cooler. The story is A and very satisfying as you further unravel the corruption behind the Italian government right down to its core, dealing with each enemy at the end of each chapter that resolves one sequence and kickstarts the next.
