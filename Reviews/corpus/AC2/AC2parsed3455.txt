Being the second Assassins Creed game, starting the trilogy of well known Ezio Auditore da Firenze. A young assassin getting revenge and becoming the leader. This game has the longest and most complex story, which if you like  you are going to enjoy the game a lot more. It has lot actions to offer, many main and side missions, random public events, etc. Controls staying almost the same as in first Assassins Creed. Vittoria agli Assassini.
