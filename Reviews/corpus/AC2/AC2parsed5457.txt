Desmond Miles is back in Assassins Creed II with another ancestral memories. This time its Ezio Auditore da Firenze, the nobleman from Renaissance Italy. Desmond will explore the life and history of his own ancestor and learn to be an assassin. Ezio is cool and interesting new character. Gameplay is improved with diverse missions and game mechanic. There are new weapons and gadgets built by master inventor, Leonardo da Vinci. Ezios life is quite drama and adventurous. Conspiracies behind the whole story is quite compelling. Game visual is great and those large Italian cities is really beautiful. Improve in almost every aspects, Assassins Creed II shows what a good sequel should be.
