This is a awesome game that really makes you feel like your an assassin that that era. Its fight style is very fluid and consistent. The graphics are wonderfully beautiful and very well done. The buildings in the game are very detailed. The story continues from Assassins Creed. It has new moves and new ways of getting around that really make the environment enjoyable to go around. Its a must have for any adventure lover! If you like the medieval era then this game would be a dream come true. Its a must have and a must play game. Its very hard to put down. I love it!
