The main thing in this game is its scenario base all major history events turn out an endless fight between assassins and templars for so called Pieces of Eden. Conspiracy, brutal violence, religious in its true shape, famous people in alternative reality  all runs through the entire game captivating till you see final credits. And, of course, its all would be nothing without a good gameplay, where you run on the city roofs of Florence, Venice, Forli and Tuscany, killing guards with variety of weapons combining them. And yes  the game is freaking long.
