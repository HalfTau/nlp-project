Historically deep and very interesting plot, full of conspiracy theories.Big, open, beautiful cities with lots of things to discover.Very fun main objectives which introduces new mechanics and fun toys .Good character development.Lots of collectibles which are actually rewarding.New economy. You can purchase equipment and more.Graphics are also pretty good for its age.VERY easy combat and stupid AI.Controls sometimes become frustrating and let you to make an unintended move such as jumping down a building or killing a civilian etc. But you get used to it as you play.Ubisoft managed to fix almost every problem with the first game and uncovered the true potential of the Assassins Creed series. ACII is the true representative of the series it is a masterpiece of its time.
