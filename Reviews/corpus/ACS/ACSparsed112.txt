Keyboard controls are a little finicky. Climbing is the most irritating. Conquering areas of the map quickly becomes tedious, but there are some areas that let you get creative with assassinations. Some of the main story missions feel repetitive, but. The side missions are the most repetitive, and I dont find myself doing them unless Im short on funds. They mostly consist of transporting a person or object, following someone or protecting an important person it seems Ubisoft loves to use filler in their games. The microtransactions that are available are for lazy folk who dont want to spend time gathering resources in game you wont have any distinct advantage over people who keep their money.
