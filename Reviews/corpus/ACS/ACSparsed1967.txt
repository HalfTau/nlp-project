After the failure that was Unity I had lost all hope in the Assassins Creed franchise ever being good again, vowed to never buy another one and basically boycott the series. When the game came on sale for a more modest price than the original CDN exchange is a  I said fine lets give this a shot. I am so glad I did this, the characters, story and location as well as the new combat and stealth mechanics feel fresh and more refined than the previous iteration and I am glad to say that this is by far the best Assassins Creed game since 2. Easy to recommend and a steal at the current price point, get it.Edit The PC port is very well done and optimized, easily able to run Ultra with a 970
