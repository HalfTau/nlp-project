aah, a classic. GTA San Andreas in the far future will make you look back and miss the good ol days of the PS2. A free roam sandbox game that has a thrilling story, cheesy dialouge,gangster wars, loads of customization and you will literally cry with laughter with times. You follow the protagonist  CJ  as he finds out his mom died, he inherits the house, is reunited with his gangster family, nagging police and glocks around the blocks  sorry i couldnt resist . Strap your low rider seat belts and load your gun, because your in for one HELL of a ride.
