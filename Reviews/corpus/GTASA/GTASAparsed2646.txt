There are already plenty of brilliant reviews for this game. I just want to add a very simple thumbs up and recommendation here as well. SAN ANDREAS is one of the finest games ever created. Pioneered and revolutionized the genre singleplayer, openworld, action, adventure. Enormous world to explore, from Los Santos, to San Fiero, to Las Venturas. Endless fun to be had. Quality writing, story and creativity, as always from ROCKSTAR. This is a mustplay game. My rating for GRAND THEFT AUTO SAN ANDREAS 10 out of 10.
