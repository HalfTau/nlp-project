A little hard to play on the pc, but still worth it. This one is a no brainer, if you like gta, you will like SA. It features 3 cities, each with their rural and urban parts. The cities are Los Santos Los Angeles, San Fierro San Diego and Las Venturas Las Vegas. You can buy houses, some business lots, clothes, change hair, modify cars, fly airplanes and helicopters, use a jetpack, tons of cheat codes, hundreds of mods and a bunch of other stuff. Features a gangster story with good twists. You will enjoy it.
