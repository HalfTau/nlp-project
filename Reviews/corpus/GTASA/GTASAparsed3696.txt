A shining example of why porting games from console to PC doesnt work. The gameplay is good, the story is very good, and the memes are dank. However the crashes are frequent, the controls are temperamental, and the graphics are blocky.A hint for any new players if youre stuck on a mission particularly if it involves water, turn on the frame limiter. For some reason the game doesnt function correctly if you run at 100fps which is not unusual considering most peoples setups.
