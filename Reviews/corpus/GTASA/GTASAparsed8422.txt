Grand Theft Auto San Andreas is the third installment in the 3D universe. It is set in the fictional city of Los Santos, where crime and gangs are rife, celebrities and policticians alike avoid the streets of Vinewood. The story in GTASA is about the tragedy of Carl Johnson, who comes back to a broken neighbourhood that has been invaded by the Ballas and the Families, their rival clans of degenerates. With his brother Smoke, childhood friend Big Smoke and Ryder, they seek to take back their hood and outlive their nightmare they lived in for so long.The gameplay in GTASA has been extended even further with the abilities from Vice City return, such as properties, assets, extraside missions including pimping, derby and racing with the inclusion of a rpglike system where your character is built upon the activities Carl Johnson does.
