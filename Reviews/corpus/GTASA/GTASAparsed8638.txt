Many great things are to be said about Grand Theft Auto San Andreas. Such a captivating storyline and the gameworld and/or gameplay is definitely one of the most fleshed out Ive seen from this particular series. Many fun and memorable hours to be found within this game which is complimented nicely by this ports fantastic controls and superior graphics.Even after all these years, this game still holds up surprisingly well and the list of songs in this game are just superb.Definitely worth a buy.
