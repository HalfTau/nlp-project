Even though this game has been published in 2004, GTA SA has most definately stood the test of time. The story is great, and the gameplay is excellent. Youll find yourself exploring the vast, fictional  state of San Andreas in any way you please. If you ever find yourself bored with the standard game, theres still a huge modding communitry thats dedicated to this game even today. With that said, this game has an impressive replay value as well as the option to mess around with cheats that will cause everyone to hate you, and track you down. Whether you want to spend countless hours flying, driving, or causing mayhem, San Andreas has something for virtually any type of gamer, and definately scores a 95/100.
