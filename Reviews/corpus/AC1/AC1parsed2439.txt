The first game in what will be an epic saga from Ubisoft. You follow Desmond Miles as hes placed into a machine known as the Animus which allows the partcipant to relive key moments of an ancestor.The ancestor in question is an Assassin called Alair in what history remembers as The Crusades. The story is rich and environments the same.This is a must buy  no hesitation. After, youll want to play its sucessors Assassins Creed II, Assassins Creed Brotherhood and the upcoming at the time of writing Assassins Creed Revelations.
