The game feels more like an extended tech demo than an actual game. The missions are repetitive, and most of the side ones  chasing flags, timed deliveries  make very little sense in the context of the story, which isnt bad, but not exactly great, either. Its still a reasonably fun and wellexecuted game. Like all AC games, its good times for a history buff, but you should still only pick up this game if youre interested in seeing how the overarching story of the series unfolds in its entirety  the following titles may be somewhat difficult to follow without knowing what happened in this one.
