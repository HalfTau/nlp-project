My blade sees for me It cuts through the darkness.This is how it all began, the journey of Desmond Miles and his Assassinancestors. You play as Altar IbnLaAhad, the assassin who founded the Order of the assassins. As Altar, you have to uncover the secrets of the creed and the mysterious apple of eden, while fighting against your enemies, the templar!It is a good game on its own, but definatly not as good as its sequels, since you cant even airassassinate people here. This game clearly isnt made for a Pc Keyboard, but for consoles, so I suggest you get a Gamepad to play with before you play this game with the frustrating controls of the Pc version.5/10
