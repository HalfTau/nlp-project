The game potrays the story fo Desmond Miles, where through a machine called the Animus is able to relive the life of his ancestor, Altar IbnLaAhad who was an assassin during the time of the Third Crusade. This game requires players to use the environment to their advantage, quite similar to another Ubisoft game franchise, The Prince of Persia. One of the main difference between this game and The Prince of Persia games is having an open world gameplay as players may choose the approach on how to fulfill the objective.
