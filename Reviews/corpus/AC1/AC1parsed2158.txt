A game with an intriguing story about the abduction of Desmond by Abstergo who is then forced into a machine called the Animus to relive the past memories of Altar ibnLaAhad during the Third Crusade, Its fantastic game play that lets you move your character, Altar, stealthily through a crowd, make stealth kills, climb, run over roofs, and admire vistas of the cities Masyaf the Brotherhoods location, Jerusalem, Acre and Damascus in an unbelievable way.Highly recommended, rated 10 out of 10.
