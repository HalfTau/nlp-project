Give it a chance and you will be glad you did. The first couple hours are a little slow because the controls are poor, but eventually you get more abilities and then the controls flow.It has a really amazing story and it is only the beginning. Buy it on sale. Dont skip this one in the series, because its definitely worth a playthrough. It will lead you into a series you will come to enjoy and love, because in the next game will take you into Ezios life and the masterpiece unravels from there.
