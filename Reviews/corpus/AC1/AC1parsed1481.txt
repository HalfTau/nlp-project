Smart stealth mechanics, given a varity of weapons to dispatch your foes and wear a cool hood.As the name suggests you play as an assassin in the time setting of the Third Crusade 1191. His name is Altar an exmaster assassin which has to relearn what it means to be an assassin and along the way uncover the secrets behind the Templar Order, ancient enemies to the Secret Order of Assassins The Creed.Slowly through out the game you get to use different weapons and get more familar with your parkour skills as you smoothly end any foe and explore the many areas and cities in the Holy Land in this era.Though some activities and missions are repetitive like racing, escorting, etc but they do get harder to do.One of the best games with an original story and unique game mechanics, this is one game you would love to play.
