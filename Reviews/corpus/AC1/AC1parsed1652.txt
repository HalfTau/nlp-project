First game in the Assassins Creed series and well worth playing even if youve already played the later ones.You need to assassinate certain targets. First, you need to gather information about your surroundings. Then, you need to gather information about your assassination target. Once you have gathered enough information, you can go and assassinate your target how you do that is up to you and the information you gathered.You can also explore the wonderful big cities and interact with many guards and civilians.
