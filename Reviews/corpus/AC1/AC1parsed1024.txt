In Assassins Creed you play as Desmond Miles, who gets kidnapped by Abstergo and shoved into what is called an Animus. This machine allows the user to revisit there ancestors past and view it how it actually happened. The reason Abstergo kidnapped Desmond in particular is because his ancestor Altair knows where The Pieces of Eden are.
