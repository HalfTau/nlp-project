A very fun revisit if you played it when you were younger, or enjoy the mechanics that games of its time werent afraid to make heavy use of. It captures something thats hard to put into words, and even harder to find a replacement for. If youre looking for deep combat or hypercustomization of your character through some talent system, stay away.Some troubleshooting may be needed to make it work on recent OSs, but a few community hub links and properties checkboxes later I have it running perfectly with win10 x64.
