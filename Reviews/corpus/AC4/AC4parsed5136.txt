Lots of people dont give this game a chance because of past failures in the franchise. However, this game not only corrects the mistakes of its predecessors, it stands on its own as a fantastic piece of interactive storytelling.The gameplay is great, the atmosphere is perfect, and the story is both fun and emotionally impactful. However, the best part about this game is the characters and dialoge. This has one of the best ensembles in the entire franchise, and everyone is wellwritten and entertaining.This game is great. Play it.
