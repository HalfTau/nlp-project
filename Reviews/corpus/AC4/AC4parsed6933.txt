Assassins Cry 4 is an adventure game where you will fight pirates, thieves and smugglers for money, filthy money.In this game you will Climb high locations to reveal sections of the map. Explore the gorgeously looking jungle environments. Fight to liberate locations and unlock upgrades. Hunt the wildlife for their bodyparts, to then craft upgrades for your gear. Kill pirates, kill many many pirates. Find money laying around through the map, and use it to buy better weapons.All in all, pretty good game. Two shark thumbs up.
