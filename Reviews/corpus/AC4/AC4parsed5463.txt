This games only flaw is being an Assassins Creed game. This wonderful gem of a colorful high seas Caribbean pirate adventure would have had volumes to gain had it not suffered from tailing quests, collectibles and tedious sequences where youre A GOD DAMNED OFFICE WORKER AT UBISOFT.Black Flag has all of the defining flaws that us Assassins Creed fans hate to love holding it down, but when its allowed to spread its wings, you better hang on to something because youre in for a ride.
