This game has one of the best graphics of its year of release. Its a beautiful graphical experience, and the sound and audio quality is just as beautiful.The world is staggeringly HUGE. The possibility of cooperating or battling with other players online is great, confuse them, make them believe youre just an NPC. The main story is pretty entertaining and lasts enough. It has a great gameplay, great weapons and customizations. The option of driving your ship in firstperson, leading it into battle is just as amazing.You can also control a fleet, use it for some side quests and earn more resources, or such.There is a huge amount of collectibles, secondary quests and challenges that will keep you busy for days.
