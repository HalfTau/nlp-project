With the open world gameplay, the feel of fresh salty sea air, the engaging and quite intrigueing story of assassins creed four you will be entertained for hours and hours on end. The naval gameplay is fantastic and the on ground combat is fluid and as fun as ever, and the interesting dual templar/assassin story on Edward is quite teh spin on the original story, adding for a nother layer of complexity and decision. And side activiites add more immersion and allow you to get lost in the true world of pirates and in this explosive world you are released into your own playground of riches and gore, all for your taking.
