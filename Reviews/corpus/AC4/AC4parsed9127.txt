The very best Assassins Creed game out there so far. Easily 50 hours of gameplay on singleplayer. If youre a completionist like myself, there is a lot to do, and you wont bore yourself out. Very expansive and seamless gameplay. Theres so much to do and youll get side tracked from the main storyline easily which isnt a bad thing. Everything is fun, and has differences in difficulties, not everything is too easy or too hard.TLDR  Vast content, seamless gameplay, many things to do, play in your own style. You control the game.10/10 considering the genre, previous Assassins Creed titles, gameplay
