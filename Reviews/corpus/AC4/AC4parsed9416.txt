An epic game though my favorite component is the sailing mechanic. This is how sail should be in a game, the ship combat and character combat flow well into each other and its easy to swap between your pistols and sword mid combat, the flow and smooth way the character moves also makes the gameplay so fun. The final aspect of all this is the story, for the first time we are playing as someone who isnt an assassin, he has his own motives he doesnt even care about the rest of the world at the beginning and thus makes a much more interesting main character.In the end this game gets an A
