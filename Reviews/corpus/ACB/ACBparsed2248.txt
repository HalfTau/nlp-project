Adult Ezio returns with even more epicness. If you enjoyed Assassins Creed 2, there is not a single reason why you should hold back from buying this one. The plethora of new features can be overwhelming at first, but once you get the hang of it you wont want to put it down. The storyline in the Assassins Creed games gets better and better as a new game is added to the series. Brotherhood is no exception. WIth the added multiplayer feature of Brotherhood, you wont be leaving your room for a while. The multiplayer adds a lot more to the game with a system that rewards you with items and perks as you level up. Buy it.
