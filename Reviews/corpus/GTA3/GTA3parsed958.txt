A true classic that introduced every gamer to a life of crime on the PlayStation 2 and / or PC. While its sucessors had improved the gameplay with each new title, its still nostalgic to go back and replay this classic. Return to Liberty City on a quest for revenge while working your way up the ladder in organized crime or just enter the Tank cheat then unlessh mayham. Meet memorable chracters like Toni Capriani, Don Leone and Donald Love while exploring the game that introduced you to the open world genre.
