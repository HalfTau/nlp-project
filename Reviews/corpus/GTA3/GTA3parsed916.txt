A long time ago, it may have been a playable product, but age looks down upon games with a mighty fist, and Grand Theft Auto 3 is no exception. Featuring incredibly poor camera controls for both vehicles and on foot, terrible shooting controls, wonky combat, lack of checkpoints or even a basic mission restart, GTA 3 should serve only as a reminder of GTAs roots or a reminder that weve come a long way since then.
