The game that turned its back on the PC and became the musthave console game of its day returned gloriously triumphant as a sprawling lifelike city where you made your own way in an open world, feeling as if you could do anything you liked. Forget the glamour of violence and get into the real world In an infamous series filled with commendable entries, GTAIII is the one to have if you want to live out your violent tendencies without the associated jail sentences. Truly a lifesaver of a game!
