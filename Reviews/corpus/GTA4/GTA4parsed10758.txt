GTA4 is a real life vehicle simulator where you are the victom who has had his vehicle stolen yet again for the 4th time and have to call out the police to file the paperwork then call your insurance adjuster to handle the situation so you can get a vehicle to make it to your job.  You have the choice of dozens of insurance companies but the one you choose to use will determine how much you get back for your vehicle and if you have a rental in the mean time which will help you get to work while you wait for them to recover yours or get a new one.  Spoiler Choose your insurance wisely and always have full coverage.
