GTA 4 is a storytelling masterpiece you and your cousin against the world. Nico is a badass man with a mission, in order to achieve the information he requires he must kill and stab his way there. throughout the story you develop reputation with certain people allowing you to further your alliance with them and bringing you closer to your goal. Not everything is as it seems here in Liberty City. You will be taken on a roller coaster ride and lose friends and make enemies. Solid 8.5/10 It would be higher but the ridiculous integration with Games for Windows and rockstars login service just makes it a hassle.
