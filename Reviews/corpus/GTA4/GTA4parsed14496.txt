Another great gta game by rockstar. I highly recomend this game if you like any other gta game. You star as niko bellic and do many jobs around liberty city. You can date on this game and go party with some contacts you have befriended. Lots of cars and motocycles to ride in. You even can get a speed boat and a helicopter to use.The only down side to this game is the helicopter controls with the keyboard/mouse. They are REALY hard to use and get used too. I tried many times. I havent tried it with a controller yet but I guess it would be alot better.
