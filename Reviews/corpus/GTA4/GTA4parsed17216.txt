What was a fantastic open world game on consoles has come to the PC in a conflicting entanglement of platforms that refuse to cooperate. I started this game hoping to quench my nostalgia, but instead I was thrown on a chase to find a working version of Microsofts Games for Windows Marketplace, after which my game finally launched. A short five minutes later I am prompted to sign in to my nonexistent Live account just to save the game. Well, there goes 5.
