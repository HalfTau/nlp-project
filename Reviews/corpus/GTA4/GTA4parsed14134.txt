GTA IV is Rockstars greatest masterpiece standing sidebyside with Red Dead Redemption. A true successor to GTA III, this game takes Liberty City and maximizes its potential with a larger world to explore, more things to do, and more fun to have. With all the delightful sidequests and crazy characters packed in every corner of the city, youll have dozens of hours of time to spend playing. Niko Bellic is the most charismatic protagonist since Tommy Vercetti, and his story is a tale worth experiencing. Also, the mod support for this is FANTASTIC! Whether you like crazy car chases, dating sims, playing minigames like darts or pool, watching strippers, getting drunk, killing everything in sight and making big money doing it, GTA IV has everything an open world actionadventure title needs.
