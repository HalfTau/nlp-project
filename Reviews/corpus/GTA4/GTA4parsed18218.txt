This openworld thirdperson action adventure stars newly illegalimmigrated Niko Bellic as he learns the ropes of life in America and attempts to make a living for himself, in many notsolegal ways. The story is excellent with fantastic voice acting. The game features a vast assortment of characters who carry many different personalities, providing the game with rich humor and solid storytelling. The shooting mechanics are excellent for a thirdperson game. The driving can take getting used to, but is decentlydone. The missions are fun and provide hours of entertainment. Outside of the main story, there are several side missions, minigames, and activities that will keep you busy for hours on end. If you havent played any GTA games before, this would be a great place to start.4.5/5
