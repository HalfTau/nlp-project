There is not much left to be said about GTA IV, or any Grand Theft Auto game really. Each one is fantastic in its own way and as a whole it is one of the best franchises in gaming. As controversial as it has been through the years, the fact remains that the satire and hard hitting issues explored within each title make it one of the the most engaging and attractive franchises.In all respects, GTA IV does not disappoint. The game is awesome and whatever few flaws you may find with it, theres a quick mod to fix it. If you dont already own a GTA game and are old enough for it to be appropriate  then pick this one up ASAP and get hyped for GTA V next year!
