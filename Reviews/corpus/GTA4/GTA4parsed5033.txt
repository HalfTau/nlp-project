A awesome Grand Theft Auto game, the story is good and lengthy. The game is perfect to mess around with on muliplayer and fun solo too. The games mechanics are fun and good, the swing glitch is really funny and amusing to mess around with. If you find some friends to play this game with, you wont regret getting it. The map is a decent size and there are many vehicles to choose from, the dlc adds more story to the game and more vehicles with is always a plus.
