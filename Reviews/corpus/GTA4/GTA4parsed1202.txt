Youre Serbian immigrant Niko Bellic, brought to Liberty City an analogue for New York by the tall tales of your cousin Roman. You become a criminal freelancer on a quest for your very own American Dream.Rob a bank with a family of Irish gangsters. Help a black rapper drunk on dreams of fame clean up the streets for his reality TV show. Guard a lesbian Puerto Rican drug dealer. Become a mercenary in the Italian Mobs civil war as the feds start closing in on them. Assassinate rivals for a Jamaican arms dealer.The writing is incredible, and Liberty City feels like home. This game has it all, and I found myself tearing up at multiple points just from the sheer humanity of it all. Even if you never play video games, you need to give this title a whirl. Itll make you feel human.
