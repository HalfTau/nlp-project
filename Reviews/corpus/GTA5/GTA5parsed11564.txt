From Racing to TDM to Heists and beyond. The things you can do in this game are awesome! Become a CEO of your own company, or a President of a Biker Club. The choice is yours how you enjoy the online gameplay. But be warned, there are always others that are trying to get what you have so play nice or naughty! Lots to check out and a whole Los Santos to enjoy. From random lines out of the NPCs or the quarkyness of creepy Lester, youll think that this Grand Theft Auto is set apart from its previous counterparts. You might be right. There is a whole new level of gameplay with this GTA.So get out there and do what you do best..... Kill/Drive/Fly.
