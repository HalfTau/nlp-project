What can I say? Its worth the money, of course youre better of waiting for a sale, but you wont regret it. The story is fantastic, has many aspects of a criminal, racing type of game. But the online mode is totally different. Meet with players, friends, start a bussiness, do heists, stunt, tune cars and meet with your friends to make a whos car is better looking contest. Of course with every game, comes a new exploit. This game has Cheaters, or Modders. They can be god mode, Kill you on respawn, or change your level Happened to me. But its cool, just find a new server, or Session.Last thoughts YOU WONT REGRET IT!
