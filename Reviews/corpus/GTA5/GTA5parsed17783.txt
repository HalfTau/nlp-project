Its the definitive game that must be played by ever RPG loving gamer. The storyline is captivating with memorable characters. The map is large but could have have been massive with additional variety and iconic wonders or landmarks. Mod support is fantastic with close to your imagination anything can be made. Multiplayer has a lot of potential and can be improved vastly. Overall one of the best games in the market there ever be and must be at least tried in your lifetime. Happy gaming!
