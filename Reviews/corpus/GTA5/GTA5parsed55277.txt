Single player is more or less just a linear story with some optional mini games thrown in. The world is large and detailed, but grows old as it has not seen an expansion since launch. The games modding community seems to be under attack by Rockstars parent company resulting in even less flexibility and single player gameplay.GTA V Online is a pay to win game portraying itself as something else. Much of the content is blocked behind large amounts of ingame currency that can only be obtained through microtransactions or by an infeasible amount of grinding.The lack of single player content since launch, the sudden drop in support for the modding community, and the recent statements from the Take2 make the companys intentions clearMoney First, Players Second.
