An open world game that is full of bad things you can do. You can do heists with your friends, you can go buy luxury things and live your life as a billionaire and criminal at the same time. There are many things you can do. However, DO NOT play alone. You will get bored 100 guaranteed. Another thing is beware of hackers giving you money, they can give you permanent bans to people receiving money like what I got from a hacker one day.
